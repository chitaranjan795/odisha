ODISHA

Odisha province was founded on the 1st April 1936, and the anniversary of this is celebrated as UTKAL DIBAS throughout the state. The earlist name of odisha was Odra, Kalinga, Utkal, Orissa and Odisha. In that way the capital of odisha was transformed from "jajpur" and "Cuttack" to "Bhubaneswar". Especially the odisha is famous for the "Temple" , "Historycal Place" and "Beaches". A lot of tourists arrives odisha cause of the Places. Odisha an eastern Indian state on the Bay of Bengal, is known for its cultures and its many ancient Hindu temples. The capital, Bhubaneswar, is home to hundreds of temples, The Lingaraj Temple complex, dating to the 11th century.
